How to play the SmartBillions lottery

You are purchasing a SmartBillions lottery ticket. Please choose your 6 lucky numbers (from 0 to 15). Keep in mind that the number order matters. Wins are paid out from 2 hits and above.

1. Go to “wallet” section
2. Create new wallet or access your existing wallet using mnemonic phrase.
3. If you create new wallet please enter your new password and save the mnemonic phrase. In order to buy lottery tickets please deposit funds to the wallet address.
4. You will be playing SmartBillions lottery from your Wallet using lottery interface.
5. You can manage funds in your wallet from “wallet” section

Use an Ethereum-enabled browser to access the SmartBillions lottery interface, i.e. Chrome + Metamask

How to PLAY using MetaMask wallet:
1. Please log in to your Metamask wallet using Chrome, Firefox, Opera or Edge
2. Choose your ticket value. Please enter a value between 0.001 – 1 ETH
3. Choose your 6 lucky numbers, each between 0-15. Please keep in mind that the number order matters. To win you must hit at least 2 numbers.
4. Click the Play Now button
5. Confirm the bet transaction
6. Wait for the transaction to be mined
7. The bet results are the hashes of the third future block
8. Play again or claim your winnings

Good Luck !!

How install the Metamask wallet :
1. Please download the Metamask wallet: https://metamask.io/
2. Enable the extension and create a new Metamask address
3. Save your private key
4. Now you can deposit ETH straight into your Metamask wallet to play the lottery!
